Road markings

You can find below all road markings and their dimension. The lane markings may be dashed or continuous and are ~2cm wide white line. The lane has a width of ~35 cm, measured from the inside of the respective markings. In the case of dashed markings, the line has a length 4.5 cm and the distance between two marks is similarly 4.5 cm.
